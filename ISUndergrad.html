Bachelor of Science in Information Systems

The BS program prepares students for all potential career positions in the IS field. These include: Systems Analysis and Design, Computer Networking and Health Care Informatics. For successful completion of the degree, all students must satisfy the general education requirements that are explained in the UMBC Undergraduate Catalog.

Information Systems:

  1. IS 300 Management Information Systems
  2. IS 310 Software and Hardware Concepts
  3. IS 410 Introduction to Database Design
  4. IS 420 Database Application Development
  5. IS 425 Decision Support Systems
  6. IS 436 Structured Systems Analysis & Design
  7. IS 450 Data Communications and Networks
  8. IS 451 Network Design and Management
  9. IS 3xx or 4xx Upper Level IS Elective (may not use IS 399, IS 400, Is 469 or a course used to satisfy an IS certificate requirement.)
